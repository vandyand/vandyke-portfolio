All three of these are multi-agent LLM apps, and they lean on the same trick:
agents that communicate through **strict JSON-schema contracts** rather than
free-form prose. That is what makes them reliable enough to chain, route, and
revise without a tangle of glue code. Each one is live — switch between them below.
