## Problem

Running multiple algorithmic trading systems across brokers means the truth
about performance is scattered across four dashboards, none of which speak
the same language. I needed a single, honest view of live performance —
returns, risk, and positions — across every venue, updated daily.

## What I built

A performance dashboard tracking four algorithmic trading systems over a
live trading period in early 2026:

- **OANDA Forex** — 20 currency instruments
- **Alpaca Equities** — US stocks
- **Solana Altmemecoins** — on-chain crypto
- **Kalshi Predictions** — event markets

The dashboard displays NAV curves, daily returns, Sharpe and Sortino
ratios, max drawdown, open positions, and risk metrics per system and in
aggregate, with TradingView-grade charting. The signals underneath are
generated by TCN (temporal convolutional network) + Actor-Critic
reinforcement-learning models with daily position sizing.

## Outcome

A single pane of glass over four live venues — every number pulled from
broker APIs, not hand-entered. It doubles as a working demonstration of the
full quant stack: model → sizing → execution → measurement, with the
measurement layer public.
