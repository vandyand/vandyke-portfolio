## Problem

Backtests lie by default: train and test on overlapping data and any
strategy looks brilliant. Walk-forward optimization is the discipline
that fixes it — train on a rolling window, test on the next unseen
period, repeat — but the results are a pile of per-window metrics
nobody can read raw.

## What I built

A dashboard that visualizes real walk-forward runs from my multi-asset
trading research system:

- **Out-of-sample equity curves** — cumulative OOS returns stitched
  across validation windows, per strategy.
- **Strategy comparison** — TCN action models (temporal convolutional
  networks over multi-timeframe features), TCN and MLP directional
  classifiers, and hand-crafted momentum/mean-reversion rules as the
  baseline, side by side.
- **Per-window detail** — Sharpe, returns, and win rate for every
  validation window, with expandable raw metrics.

The underlying models are PyTorch; the dashboard renders curated run
results — no inference in the app, just honest measurement.

## Outcome

A research tool that makes the train/test boundary visible instead of
hiding it. It pairs with the [live trading
dashboard](/projects/avd-trading-dashboard): this is where strategies
prove themselves out-of-sample before any of them touch a broker.
